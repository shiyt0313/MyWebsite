## Overview

Typical speech interfaces require users to either speak loudly, disturbing nearby people, or use near-mouth devices, which
are often socially inappropriate.

## Contribution

WhisperBuds explores how commodity earbuds can support low-error whisper input and more discreet voice interaction in
everyday settings.

## Why it matters

This project fits the broader research agenda around wearable sensing, practical interaction design, and more socially
acceptable AI-enabled interfaces.

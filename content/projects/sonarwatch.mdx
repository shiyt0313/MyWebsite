## Overview

SonarWatch uses the acoustic field generated by transceivers on opposite sides of a watch to detect nearby objects and
infer interaction opportunities.

## Contribution

The project combines IMU and acoustic sensing to support same-side and opposite-side hand interactions with attention to
power efficiency.
